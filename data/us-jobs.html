⚡ Why Cap-Exempt Matters for You

  • No lottery: File H-1B year-round — not just April. Zero luck required.
  • Universities & community colleges: Cap-exempt by statute (INA §214(g)(5)(A))
  • State/county/city/transit government: All cap-exempt by statute
  • 501(c)(3) nonprofits affiliated with universities or engaged primarily in research: Cap-exempt (INA §214(g)(5)(C))
  • DOE National Labs: Managed by nonprofits (UT-Battelle, UC, Battelle) — cap-exempt, no citizenship req for unclassified roles
  • $100K fee does NOT apply if you change status from F-1 while inside the US
