## NETHERLANDS VISA/SPONSORSHIP CONTEXT

### How It Works
- The Netherlands has a **Highly Skilled Migrant (Kennismigrant)** visa
- The employer must be a **recognized sponsor (erkend referent)** registered with the IND
- The IND maintains a public list of recognized sponsors: https://ind.nl/en/public-register-recognised-sponsors
- Processing time is typically 2-4 weeks after employer files
- Mohamad qualifies: Master's degree, 5 years experience, under 30 (if applicable for reduced salary threshold)

### Salary Threshold (2025/2026)
- For workers age 30+: approximately EUR 5,008/month gross (check current IND thresholds)
- For workers under 30: approximately EUR 3,672/month gross (reduced threshold)
- For graduates of Dutch top universities within 3 years: search year visa option (not applicable here but included for context)
- **Any job offer must meet the salary threshold for the HSM visa to be granted**

### Key Point
- The company does NOT need to be a big name. Any company on the IND recognized sponsor list can sponsor
- Many small/medium Dutch tech companies, agencies, and consultancies are recognized sponsors
- Startups that have gone through the process before are good targets
- Companies that already have international employees are more likely to sponsor
- Even staffing/consultancy firms (detachering) that place developers at clients can sponsor
