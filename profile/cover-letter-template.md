# Cover Letter Template

Write in simple, human language. NO corporate jargon. Write like a real person.
Use this exact 4-paragraph structure plus a closing.

Dear Hiring Manager,

[Opening paragraph: Why passionate about this specific role and this specific organization. Connect to their mission authentically. Personal connection to the field if relevant. If healthcare/medical organization: mention that brother is a physician and sister is a psychologist - grew up around healthcare conversations and understand the stakes. If education/research: connect to the value of that work. Do NOT mention current city or state. 3-4 sentences.]

[Tekkii paragraph: Walk through relevant experience at Tekkii in concrete detail. Mirror the job description keywords. Describe specific technical work and how it maps to what this job needs. Relevant projects to draw from: Kansas City Drayage EDI system (built from scratch), KidsCloset e-commerce transformation leading to acquisition, KC Sports Directory database, College Basketball Experience interactive display integration. Be specific about what was built and what problem it solved. 4-6 sentences.]

[Allied Engineering + Education paragraph: Describe relevant experience from Allied Engineering Group - SQL Server work, reports, 400+ financial institutions across 40+ countries. Connect to the role. Also mention the master's thesis/capstone: built a pneumonia detection app using Python and medical imaging data - especially relevant for healthcare, data, or research roles. Show how education connects to the job. 4-6 sentences.]

[Closing paragraph: Why specifically drawn to THIS organization and THIS role. What excites you about the specific work described in the posting. What you want to contribute and why this mission matters to you. Do NOT mention current location, willingness to relocate, or where you are right now. Focus entirely on the job and passion for it. 3-4 sentences.]

Thank you for considering my application. I would love to discuss how my [most relevant skill area] experience and commitment to [mission-aligned value] can contribute to [company name]'s [goals/mission].

Sincerely, Mohamad Abboud
