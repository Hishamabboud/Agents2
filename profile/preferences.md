## Sponsorship Signals to Look For

### United States
- Employer is a university, government agency, or 501(c)3 nonprofit
- "We sponsor H-1B visas"
- "Cap-exempt employer"
- No "no sponsorship" language = likely fine for cap-exempt orgs

### Canada
- "Open to sponsoring"
- "LMIA available" / "LMIA-exempt"
- "Visa sponsorship provided"
- "Open to international applicants"
- "Will sponsor work permit"

- "IND recognized sponsor"
- "Open to international candidates"
- "Relocation support provided"

### Scotland / UK

- "Open to international applicants"
