Across all of these projects, my focus was the same: make data trustworthy and make it useful for the people who need it.

I also earned my Master of Science in Computer Science from the University of Missouri-Kansas City, where one of my capstone projects was a pneumonia detection application built in Python using medical imaging data. That project sits right at the intersection of clinical data and practical technology, and it deepened my commitment to building tools that support better health outcomes.

What draws me to AllHealth Network specifically is that you are doing this work in behavioral and mental health, an area that is historically underserved by strong data infrastructure. I am excited about the opportunity to build a clinical data platform that clinicians actually want to use, to lead a team of analysts focused on meaningful outcomes, and to help translate complex data into clearer decisions at every level of your organization. This is the kind of role where technical skill and human purpose come together, and that is exactly where I want to be.

Thank you for considering my application. I would love to discuss how my experience in data architecture, clinical data analysis, and cross-functional stakeholder collaboration can contribute to AllHealth Network's mission of supporting the well-being of the communities you serve.
