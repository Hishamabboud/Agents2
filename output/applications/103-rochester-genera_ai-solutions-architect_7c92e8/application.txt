This role is exciting to me because it sits right at the intersection of technical architecture and real clinical impact. The work of moving AI use cases from discovery through to production deployment, with proper governance, PHI safeguards, and human-in-the-loop controls, is exactly the kind of problem I want to be working on. Rochester Regional Health is clearly approaching AI seriously and thoughtfully, and I want to be part of a team that is building something that actually helps patients and the people who care for them.

Thank you for considering my application. I would love to discuss how my experience in data architecture, system integration, and healthcare-focused machine learning can contribute to Rochester General Hospital's mission of delivering safe, reliable, and meaningful AI solutions.
